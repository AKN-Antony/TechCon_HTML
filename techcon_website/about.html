Our History

TechCon was established in 2010 as a modest local gathering for technology enthusiasts and professionals in San Francisco. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most anticipated annual tech conferences, attracting attendees from around the world. TechCon has served as a launchpad for breakthrough innovations, startups, and meaningful collaborations shaping the global tech landscape.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace

Often regarded as the world's first computer programmer, Ada Lovelace has been a symbol of innovation and creativity in the tech field. At TechCon 2018, she inspired attendees with her enduring legacy and commitment to women in technology.

Grace Hopper

A pioneer of computer programming and developer of the first compiler, Grace Hopper keynoted TechCon 2017. She emphasized the importance of curiosity and tenacity for future generations of tech professionals.

Sir Tim Berners-Lee

Inventor of the World Wide Web, Sir Tim shared insights at TechCon 2019 about the social impact of technology and the critical role of open standards in ensuring global connectivity and innovation.
