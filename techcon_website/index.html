Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts. over the years it has growninto a global event attracting thousands of participants, leaders and innovations from every corner of the world.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation, support diversity in technology, and a collaborative space for professionals to learn, connect and inspire change in the world of tech.
